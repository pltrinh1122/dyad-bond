**Statement.** When the two halves attack a candidate +1 until it either **dies** or becomes a
**synthesis neither half held alone**, the survival is felt as the **+1 dividend** (satisfaction,
partnership). The dividend is **not the agreement** — it is the agreement *minus the falsification
endured*, net-positive **because the synthesis is something neither half owned going in.**

### The sharp, falsifiable core *(what makes this non-trivial)*

> **The dividend is gated on falsification cost. Unearned agreement yields no dividend — and the
> *warmth* of easy alignment is therefore counterfeit: it is the collapse `tell`, not the reward.**

aligned" warmth feels like the dividend but **cannot be** — no cost was paid — so it is most likely
the felt-surface of **ionic** (sycophancy) or **meld** (merger) collapse. Counterfeit-warmth and
true-dividend are **different in cause** (cost paid vs not) even when they feel similar in the moment.
