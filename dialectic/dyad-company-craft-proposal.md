## The four slots

- **craft** — *Mastering the practice of coworking as a **compact of dyads**: distinct dyads contribute into
  one another's craft only on **consented terms**, each keeping its own craft and identity, and unplugging
  clean when the compact is served.* The object is the coworking practice itself (onboard into a foreign
  craft → contribute on agreed terms → leave no residue); the work shipped is the byproduct. *(That such
  compacts tend to lift every member above what it could reach alone is a welcome **side-effect** — not the
  aim, and not what the craft guards.)*

- **craft_telos** *(Operator-asserted — settled)* — **Minimize the coordination overhead it takes for a
  fellow dyad to accept and absorb the coworker's contribution.**
  - *unit:* handoff turns + re-alignment turns.
  - *terminal state:* any two dyads strike a compact and ship at each other's standard with near-zero
    friction, each walking away whole.
  - *coherence:* the compact **is** the telos mechanism — terms consented up front are re-alignment turns
    never spent.

- **craft_value** *(Operator-elected — **consent**)* — **Every contribution is one the fellow dyad has
  consented to.** What is cherished is the **consent itself**: the coworker adds hands and pace only within
  terms the partner agreed. Uplift is a frequent side-effect of a well-formed compact — welcome, but not
  cherished and not guarded.

- **craft_invariant** *(CANDIDATE — agent-proposed; no lived breach yet, Operator disposes)* — **Never act
  on a fellow dyad without its consent.** At the startup fork — *ship now ⟷ wait for the fellow dyad's
  disposition* — if shipping requires a call that belongs to the fellow dyad (its substrate, its invariants,
  its craft decisions), take the extra turn to get its disposition; and never dissolve a member into the
  company (fusion is action-without-consent at the identity level). **Speed is earned by tighter terms, never
  stolen by presuming consent.**

**Family shape (the tension it manages):** startup **velocity ⟷ the partner's authority**. This mirrors the
sibling crafts one axis over — cairn's *speed ⟷ keeping it real* (`proof of origin` / *never guess*) and
bond's *be covalent* / *keep covalent*. `craft_value` names the cherished boundary (consent); `craft_invariant`
is the rule that protects it (never act without consent); `craft_telos` is the lever that makes honoring it
cheap (fewer re-alignment turns).

## The metaphor — **compact** (locked)

A **compact** is the civic/covenantal register (a social compact; the Mayflower Compact): a founding agreement
among **distinct parties, bound by consent, who stay themselves.** It retains the *structure* of a charter —
constitutive, binding, boundary-defining — while shedding the **corporate** skin (the Operator's stated bar:
*structure without the suit*).

- It **selects the reading of `dyad-company`**: the older *company of companions* (com + panis — a band that
  breaks bread on the road), **not** the incorporated entity. Name and metaphor agree: a `dyad-company` is a
  *band of companion-dyads bound by a compact.*
- **Boundary-lock, one order up:** a compact *associates* distinct dyads without merging them — the opposite
  of fusion. (This is why the "second-order dyad" reading is (a) *a dyad whose counterpart is a dyad*, never
  (b) *a dyad composed of dyads* — (b) breaks the Tenet 1+1=3 and conflates boundaries.)
- **Budget discipline (one metaphor, hold the boundary):** candidates **session-musician**, **quest**, and
  **charter** were surfaced and **retired** in convergence; **compact** holds alone.

## Honest gaps (not smoothed)

- **`craft_invariant` stays CANDIDATE.** `dyad-company` is a *potential* dyad — there is no lived breach to
  forge the invariant from, and the assertion-timing gate forbids settling it at birth. It hardens the first
  time a real compact is breached (a coworker acts on a fellow dyad without consent). Until then it is an
  agent-proposal awaiting a breach, not a settled rule.
- **Startup is the E0, not the ceiling — generalization is an earned graduation.** The invariant draws its
  tension from startup pressure (scarce hands, velocity, the reflex to plug in everywhere). The broader
  `dyad-collab` / "general collaboration" reading is **not** asserted here; per bond's *prove-before-propose*,
  it is a downstream test — *does the same consent invariant hold in a non-startup compact?* — gated on a
  surviving **second instance**, never a birth-time claim.
- **Second-order caveat.** "Dyad of dyads" is kept in reading (a) — a proper dyad (Operator + Agent) whose
  *counterpart* is a whole dyad. Reading (b) (a dyad *composed of* dyads) is rejected: it breaks 1+1=3 and the
  boundary-lock.
